More Than You Wanted to Know: and other stories.

Philip J. Dolan, Jr. takes an opportunity to reflect on his experiences throughout his life in a compilation of works. His autobiography addresses the difficulties he's overcome, the great joys he's endured, and the extensive family he's become the patriarch to. Beyond his autobiography, Phil delivers a glimpse at the environment and culture he was raised in, a deep look into his family's unique history, and offers all readers one-of-a-kind advice and wisdom. From "Two Brothers," the story about the reconciliation of half-brothers who lived for over 75 years without meeting, to "A View From the Top," a series of memoirs with perspective and guidance, this collection from Phil is a powerful journey from cover to cover.
